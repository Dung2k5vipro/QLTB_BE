# SKILL: build-crud

Muc tieu: Tao CRUD nhat quan giua route, controller, service, repository.

Quy tac:
- `route`: khai bao endpoint REST, map sang controller.
- `controller`: nhan request, goi service, tra response.
- `service`: xu ly nghiep vu, khong truy cap DB truc tiep neu da co repository.
- `repository`: chi tap trung thao tac du lieu/SQL.
- Dat ten ham theo hanh dong ro rang: `create`, `findAll`, `findById`, `update`, `remove`.
- Tach ro validate, auth, business logic de de bao tri.
